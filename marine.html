This dashboard allows researchers to upload underwater video footage. The video will be sent to a YOLO-based deep learning model trained to detect and classify various species of fish and marine organisms.

Once processed, the system will generate a detailed report showing:

  • Species detected
  • Count per species
  • Time and frame of detection
  • Confidence level

The generated report will be available for download and can also be sent to your email.
